WHAT IS HYDRO JETTING?

Hydro jetting is a professional drain and sewer cleaning method that uses high-pressure water to completely clear the inside of pipes. Unlike snaking, which only punches through a blockage, hydro jetting scrubs the entire pipe wall clean.
